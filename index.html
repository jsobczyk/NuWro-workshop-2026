NuWro Workshop

A focused meeting on the modelling of neutrino interactions and on improving physics models for implementation in modern Monte Carlo generators.

Participation by invitation only.

Date 16–18 September 2026
Venue University of Wrocław
Room Room 422
Format Invitation-only workshop

Scope

Beyond a single generator

The workshop uses the NuWro name, but its scientific scope is deliberately broader.

Workshop goal

Although the workshop bears the NuWro name, its scope will be broader, focusing on general aspects of modelling neutrino interactions.

The reference to NuWro in the title reflects a broader goal of the neutrino interaction community: the development of improved models suitable for implementation in Monte Carlo generators, thereby supporting the success of current and future neutrino oscillation experiments.

At the same time, the workshop will provide an excellent opportunity to present recent efforts to enhance the quality of the physics models implemented in NuWro.
