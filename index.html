NuWro Workshop 2026

Although the workshop bears the NuWro name, its scope will be broader, focusing on general aspects of modeling neutrino interactions. The reference to NuWro in the title reflects a broader goal of the neutrino interaction community: the development of improved models suitable for implementation in Monte Carlo generators, thereby supporting the success of current and future neutrino oscillation experiments. At the same time, the workshop will provide an excellent opportunity to present recent efforts to enhance the quality of the physics models implemented in NuWro.

Date: 16–18 September 2026

Co-chair: Natalie Jachowicz, Xianguo Lu, Jan Sobczyk

Venue: Wrocław, Poland
